What is paper helicopter?

Paper helicopter is a modification of paper into a helicopter propeller with the following components.




The components are as follows.

1.Wing

It is the most important part for slowing down.

2.Body

It is connected to both wings and affects the speed of fall as well.

3.Center of gravity

Helps to fall by clamping it to the other end of the wing axis.
